Recycling

The burden on the environment caused by electronic devices (e-waste) is estimated at 50 million tons per year. According to globalwaste.org " E-waste is one of the fastest growing and most complex waste streams in the world, affecting both human health and the environment, and proliferating a loss of valuable raw materials". To reduce your e-waste you can bring the broken device to us. We will take the working electronic parts to be reused. Plastic and non-functional electronic parts will be recycled in the respective bins. For more information please contact us.
